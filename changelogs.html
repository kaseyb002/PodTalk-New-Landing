v 1.1.0 - (In Review)

New themes

You can chose between several new themes and mark them as your prefered light/dark theme to replace the default ones.

A lot more options

The "preference" page was rebuilt from scratch to add a lot of new options: download comments in advance, expand discussion by default, disable the reader, ... What else could you possibly want? (No but seriously, tell me.)

Fix bugs

Fixed several bugs on the "Lists" tab.

  • New options:
    • 2 new themes: Reader Dark and Reader Light. You can select you preferred light and dark themes. You can then manually switch between them or let the app pick the right one for you.
    • New list layout style: dense layout. You can choose the default style in the options or switch between them with the toggle on the top of the news list on the home page.
    • Disable the automatic reader, open directly the webpage instead
    • Unfold all the comments by default
    • Disable the rearrangement of the comment section when a comment is expanded: by default the comment section is scrolled to make the first reply visible.
    • Contribution guide + contact form
  • Bug fixes:
    • Cut text on the login form
    • Empty state placeholder that wasn't disappearing in the "Submissions" list
    • Fixed sort filters in the lists (they didn't work)
    • Load more than 30 bookmarks & recently read news (in the lists)
